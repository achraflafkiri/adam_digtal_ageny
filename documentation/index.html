1. Installation

  1. Download: Upon purchase, download the Digito zip file from your TemplateMonster account.
  2. Extract: Unzip the downloaded file to access the template files.
  3. Upload Files: Use FTP or your hosting provider's file manager to upload the extracted files to your web server.
  4. Access: Visit your domain in a web browser to access the installed Digito template.

2. Customization

Digito offers extensive customization options. Here's how to personalize your website:

  1. Assets: Replace assets (images, css, fonts, js) in the assets folder with your own media files.
  2. Content: Edit HTML files in the root directory to update content, including text and links.
  3. Navigation: Modify navigation links in the index.html file to align with your website structure.

3. Page Structure

Digito follows a structured layout. Here's a brief overview:

  1. Header: Contains the logo and navigation menu.
  2. Hero Section: The main introductory section with a headline and call-to-action button.
  3. Features: Section highlighting key features or services offered by your agency.
  4. About: Section detailing information about your agency, mission, and values.
  5. Portfolio: Showcase your agency's portfolio or featured projects.
  6. Team: Introduce your team members in this section.
  7. Testimonials: Display client testimonials to build credibility.
  8. Pricing: Section displaying pricing plans for your services.
  9. Blog: Share industry insights or company updates in this blog section.
  10. Contact: Footer section with contact information and a contact form.
